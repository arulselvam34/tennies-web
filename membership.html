Membership

Individual Tennis Junior or Adult membership:

$40/month:

  • Complimentary usage of courts for the individual player (does not include league play). Please txt (713)3927341 to reserve the courts.
  • Discounted rate for match play/tournaments/camps/drills.
  • Members can bring guests for $5/person/hour. Please venmo the court fee @klugetennis before court usage
  • Participate in our group/private lessons.
Family Tennis membership:

$60/month :

  • Members can bring guests for $5/person/hour. Please venmo the court fee @klugetennis before court usage

Policies

  • If you have any questions about any of our policies, please feel free to contact us at the email or phone numbers below.
  • Payment for classes is due the 1st of every month. All payment should be made through auto payment. A valid credit card should be in file for auto payment. A convenience fee of $25 will be charged for any failed transactions.
  • A one-time $45 registration fee is due with the first month's payment. If lessons are stopped for 1 month or more, the registration fee will be due again upon rejoining.
  • Please pick up your children within 10 minutes of the ending time of the class your child is attending.
  • We love having all of our kids at practice. However, we ask that if your child has been sick, please keep them at home until they have been fever/sickness free for at least twenty-four hours.
  • Tuition will only be prorated when joining after the 1st week of the month.
  • In the event of inclement weather an email will be sent if we cancel the class.
  • If you refer another student to Kluge, please let us know as you will receive a $30 credit on your tuition the following month.
  • If you take private lessons and need to cancel one, please notify the coach 24 hrs prior to that to avoid being charged for the lesson
  • Please participate in discarding cups and papers in the trash
  • Tuition and registration fees are non-refundable.
